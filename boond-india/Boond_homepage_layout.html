The craft

Handcrafted in
Kannauj —
India's perfume capital

Made using the ancient Deg-Bhapka distillation technique, unchanged for over a thousand years.

Employing 15+ local artisans. Every bottle handcrafted, every label screen-printed by hand.

Conferred with the GI Tag by the Government of India for authentic Kannauj Perfume.
